Return Policy

7-Day Cash Return Policy: 7-day period from the date of purchase during which customers can request a return for eligible courses. If you are unsatisfied with the course, you may be eligible for a full refund of the purchase price.

Refund Process: To initiate a return, Customers should contact IT Center Inc.'s customer support via email, phone, or an online support system. The customer support team will guide you through the return process, verify eligibility, and initiate the refund if you need it.

Exclusions: Certain courses or services may not be eligible for a refund, such as courses that have already been completed or expired, personalized courses, or courses that were part of a bundled deal with non-refundable items.

Processing Time: After approval, refunds typically take a few business days to be processed. The time it takes for the refunded amount to reflect in your account may vary depending on the payment method used during the initial purchase
